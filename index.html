ABOUT

Ricky R. Nazareth, known on stage as SANCTÜS, is a 22yrs old Jaipur-based DJ reshaping the soundscape with a fusion of global underground energy and nostalgic soul. His sets weave through Afro, House, Techno, Drum & Bass, Afro, Grime HipHop, and alot Latin rhythms, layered with the timeless charm of Bollywood/Punjabi classics and melodies. Blending deep emotion with high-energy flow, SANCTÜS transforms nostalgia into movement, where vintage meets modern, and every beat tells a story.

My Sound

Began as pure fascination for the energy of Skrillex, Diplo, David Guetta, and Avicii, it slowly evolved into a deeper exploration of sounds. As he grew, his taste shifted towards House and Afro, inspired by artists like Black Coffee and Keinemusik, and then into the raw pulse of Drum & Bass, Jungle, and UK Garage through Chase & Status, Shy FX, Sub Focus, DJ Marky, and Interplanetary Criminal.

Lately, the soulful textures of Fred Again.., Hugel, James Hype, Yosuke Yokomatsu, and Sammy Virji continue to shape his sound. Now, under the name SANCTÜS, he creates fusion sets that blend every flavour, from global underground to Bollywood and Punjabi nostalgia. Each performance is a mix of emotion and energy, a space where memories meet movement, and rhythm bridges cultures.
